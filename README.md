EDUCATIONAL_VALUE/electricity_safety.md
Safety Rules
1. Do not handle live electricity without proper supervision.
2. Do not sell or demonstrate unsafe electrical items.
3. Keep all educational demonstrations low-risk.
4. Verify materials before public use.
5. Document what is being shown, sold, or taught.

EVENT_FRAMEWORK/green_light_requirements.md
Green Light Requirements
Before the event proceeds, the following must be clear:
* Who approved it
* What is being sold or demonstrated
* Where it is happening
* Who is responsible
* What safety rules apply
* Whether church friends are attending, helping, or co-hosting
* Whether the event is educational, fundraising, comedy-based, or mixed-use
Until those are answered, the repo status remains:
Waiting for green light.
